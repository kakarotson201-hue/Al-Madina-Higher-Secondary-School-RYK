About AMHSC

Our History

Al-Madina Higher Secondary School was initially established by Mufti Abdul Wahid Awan in 1968. It was officially reopened and modernized in 2022 by his eldest son, Allama Muhammad Ahmad Awan.

Since its inception, thousands of students have memorized the Holy Quran. Today, we proudly offer modern education alongside Hifz-ul-Quran, ensuring a balanced and comprehensive foundation for our students in Rahim Yar Khan and beyond.

Message of Patron-in-Chief

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world. At Al-Madina, we strive to equip our students with both spiritual grounding and worldly knowledge."

Message of Principal

"Our faculty is dedicated to nurturing the individual potential of every student, ensuring they grow into responsible, educated, and ethical citizens."
